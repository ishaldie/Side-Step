# Side Step - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Side Step ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Game Progress:** Level completion, stars earned, coins collected, and shoes purchased are stored locally on your device.
- **Settings:** Your audio preferences and tutorial completion status are stored locally.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** We may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting purposes.
- **Usage Data:** We collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App, including levels played, session duration, and in-app purchases.

### Third-Party Services

Our App may use the following third-party services that collect information:

1. **Google AdMob** - For displaying advertisements
   - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
   
2. **Apple App Store** - For processing in-app purchases
   - Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

3. **Firebase Analytics** (if enabled) - For usage analytics
   - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Save your game progress locally
- Display relevant advertisements
- Process in-app purchases
- Improve the App experience
- Fix bugs and technical issues

## Data Storage

All game progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not store personal information on external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Side Step is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect names, emails, or personal identifiers
- Displays only age-appropriate advertisements (G-rated)

## Advertising

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. These ads may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant content. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all local game data by uninstalling the App
- Reset your game progress within the App settings
- Opt out of personalized advertising in device settings
- Request information about data we collect by contacting us

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
